TUNES BY THE HOUR transforms time into a dynamic audiovisual experience. With every passing hour, the website reflects the evolving moods of the day. The curated playlist shifts, seamlessly transitioning between genres to match the ambiance of the hour.

The central circle on the screen serves as a visual indicator, morphing in color to synchronize with the mood. From upbeat rhythms to soothing melodies, one can embark on a journey through time.

Whether seeking motivation in the morning or tranquility at dusk, TUNES BY THE HOUR delivers a harmonious blend of music and mood.
